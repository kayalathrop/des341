Whatif 006
by Shel Silverstein









Last night, while I lay thinking here,

some Whatifs crawled inside my ear

sand pranced and partied all night long

sand sang their same old Whatif song:

W

    I'm dumb in school?

    they've closed the swimming pool?

    I get beat up?

H

    there's poison in my cup?

    I start to cry?

    I get sick and die?

A

    I flunk that test?

    green hair grows on my chest?

    nobody likes me?

T

    a bolt of lightning strikes me?

    I don't grow talle?

    my head starts getting smaller?

I

    the fish won't bite?

    the wind tears up my kite?

    they start a war?

    my parents get divorced?

F

    the bus is late?

    my teeth don't grow in straight?

    I tear my pants?

    I never learn to dance?

Everything seems well, and then

sthe nighttime Whatifs strike again!
