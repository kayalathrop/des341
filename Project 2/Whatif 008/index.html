Whatif 008
by Shel Silverstein






Last
night,
while
I
lay
thinking
here,

some
Whatifs
crawled
inside
my

ear
and
pranced
and
partied

all
night
long
and
sang

their
same
old
Whatif
song:


Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif Whatif


I'm dumb in school?
they've closed the swimming pool?
I get beat up?
there's poison in my cup?
I start to cry?

I get sick and die?
I flunk that test?
green hair grows on my chest?
nobody likes me?
a bolt of lightning strikes me?

I don't grow talle?
my head starts getting smaller?
the fish won't bite?
the wind tears up my kite?
they start a war?


my parents get divorced?
the bus is late?
my teeth don't grow in straight?
I tear my pants?
I never learn to dance?


Everything seems well, and then the nighttime

Whatifs

strike again!
